Dr. Norman Borlaug

1914-2009

The man who saved a billions lives.

Dr. Norman Borlaug with his friends

Here is a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914 - Born in Cresco, Iowa.
  • 1933 - Leaves his family's farm to attend the University of Minnesota.
  • 1935 - Has to stop the school to save money.
  • 1938 - Finishes the university.
  • 1967 - Visits the UK.
  • 2009 - Dies at the ages of 9..

Dr. Norman Borlaug: Civilization as it is known today could not have evolved, nor can it survive, without an adequate food supply.
